About Us


Who we are?

Shri Shiv Kumar Kedia, the founder and promoter of Kedia Homes has been a pioneering name in the construction industry since last 37+ years and this family heritage has been pursued by his dear sons. Kedia homes has evolved into one of the most well-known and credible names in the housing industry as a result of its consistent pioneering efforts. For over three decades, this family-run company has been a reliable name. Kedia Homes began its journey as colonisers, making significant strides in the area for a long time before expanding into the field of building and construction. Through persistent and never ending efforts Kedia homes has over 300 townships and residential projects in Jaipur and more than one lakh happy and satisfied customers.
